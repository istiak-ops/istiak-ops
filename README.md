### 👨‍💻 A bit about me
I am a **Platform and Cloud Engineer** specializing in Kubernetes, GitOps, and building Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs). Leveraging a unique background in business operations, I don't just write automation scripts—I design infrastructure that directly drives ROI. 

My engineering focus is on reducing cloud costs, eliminating operational bottlenecks, and providing developers with secure, self-service, zero-drift environments. Currently, I am actively bridging the gap between traditional Cloud-Native infrastructure and the future of **Agentic Ops (LLMOps)** by building AI-driven operational workflows.

---

### ⚡ What I'm currently working on
- 🤖 **Architecting an Agentic IDP:** Building an AI-driven platform copilot using **Go, Python, and LangGraph** to automate infrastructure provisioning via the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**.
- ☸️ **GitOps & Control Planes:** Designing zero-drift deployment pipelines and internal cloud APIs using **ArgoCD, Terraform, and Crossplane** on AWS & Azure.
- 📡 **Observability & Search:** Implementing full-stack telemetry and centralized logging with **Prometheus, Grafana, and Elasticsearch** to drive predictive scaling.
